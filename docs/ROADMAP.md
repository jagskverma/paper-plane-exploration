## Key Milestones

### Milestone 1: Beautiful Flight in Empty Space
Phases 1–4 combined deliverable.
Paper plane flies through atmospheric void. Movement feels good. Atmosphere is emotionally compelling. No terrain, no world — just sky and flight.

**Gate:** If this is not achieved, do not proceed to terrain.

### Milestone 2: Spherical World Traversal
Phases 5–6 combined deliverable.
Paper plane traverses a cube-sphere planet. Seamless. Chunks stream. Scale illusion holds up.

### Milestone 3: Living World
Phases 7–12 combined deliverable.
Terrain, biomes, weather, landmarks. The world feels varied and worth exploring. 10–20 minutes of traversal before repetition.

### Milestone 4: Complete Experience
Phases 13–18 combined deliverable.
Audio, polish, performance, packaging. Ship-ready.
