# Vision

## Emotional Target

> the feeling of drifting through a vast beautiful world

The player controls a paper plane flying around a large finite spherical world. The world should feel large, mysterious, varied, and emotionally expansive — without requiring true planetary scale or infinite terrain.

The intended experience is closer to atmospheric exploration, meditative traversal, and visual wandering than objective-driven gameplay.

## What This Project IS

- A relaxing, dreamlike, atmospheric flying experience
- An exploration-driven traversal game
- A mood-first, visually expressive world
- Emotionally immersive

## What This Project IS NOT

- A hardcore flight simulator
- A combat game
- A systems-heavy survival game
- A content-bloated open-world RPG
- A generic procedural sandbox
- A tech demo
- A survival crafting game
- A feature-heavy systems project

## Core Pillars

1. **Movement feel** — smooth, gliding, momentum-based, calming, expressive
2. **Atmosphere** — sky, fog, haze, lighting, color grading, mood
3. **Traversal pleasure** — the act of flying should be intrinsically satisfying
4. **Scale illusion** — perceived scale through atmosphere, not literal size
5. **Exploration curiosity** — varied biomes, landmarks, environmental storytelling

## Visual Direction

- Stylized lowpoly visuals
- Simplified geometry
- Strong atmospheric rendering
- Soft gradients
- Large silhouettes
- Exaggerated environmental scale

Intentionally avoids photorealism, ultra-detailed assets, texture-heavy rendering, and realistic simulation fidelity.

Visual emphasis: sky, fog, horizon depth, silhouette readability, color scripting, mood — over geometric density.

## Flight Philosophy

The flying experience is the emotional core. More important than terrain early on.

Flight should feel: smooth, gliding, momentum-based, calming, expressive, atmospheric.

Key areas: banking turns, momentum smoothing, camera damping, horizon stability, traversal flow, emotional movement feel.

Avoid: arcade jet behavior, twitch controls, overly realistic aerodynamics, aggressive simulation complexity.

## Guiding Principle

> Emotional coherence over technical maximalism.

The goal is a memorable atmospheric flying experience — not the most advanced browser procedural engine.
